Instructions for Zha:

- In Zha we have 2 lives represented by our 2 hands. The game is played in a smiliar fashion to rock-paper-scissors in which we are using rock, human or gun as our moves instead.
- Rock beats Gun, Gun beats Human and Human beats Rock.
- During a player's turn, he is on the offensive side and will not lose any lives while the opposing player who is being attacked is vulnerable to losing lives.
- When a player loses a life, he loses one hand and can only play one move for subsequent turns.
- When a player loses both lives he loses the game.
- Whichever player starts first will be determined by a simple game of rock-paper-scissors and subsequent turns will alternate between players.
